what we do

Our Services

01

Trade/
Distribution

Through a thorough understanding and experience in the eco-friendly energy industry, we provide seamless support for the entire process of renewable energy material production requests, manufacturing (OME), import, delivery, and follow-up management. Additionally, we can immediately address quality, delivery, and transportation issues, offering reasonable solutions. We have overcome the drawbacks of overseas transactions, such as difficulty in returns and purchase cancellations, and turned them into advantages.

02

Plastic Resin
Toll Processing

We manage plastic resin processing and domestic deliveries, as well as exports, through a contract manufacturing agreement with Lotte Sambak LFT Co., Ltd. The entire process, including PP resin receipt, production, packaging, storage, delivery, and export, is systematically managed by personnel with extensive experience and training. Particularly for domestic shipments, we achieve the shortest delivery times and can respond to urgent orders, minimizing the company's risks

03

Non-ferrous Metal
Storage

We provide storage facilities that match the types and characteristics of logistics, manage inbound and outbound operations, and conduct regular inventory checks, reporting the results to customers. We offer high-quality services and various benefits relative to rental costs, and have built a structure that allows for quick and accurate responses even to small volumes. This has led to high customer satisfaction and the ability to attract long-term clients.

04

Solar Project Services

Through consulting by experts with know-how and strategies, we provide differentiated services for the most efficient solar power generation projects in the country, from installation to maintenance. We offer a systematic service that provides tangible value to our clients through customer consultations, design, installation, supervision, and maintenance.
